About Me

Who I Am

Professional Background

Before transitioning into technical writing, I served as a Senior Scientist for an international oil producer. In this role, I managed all aspects of planning, project management, operations, and application. Additionally, I engaged in private biosensor research and development, where I communicated complex technical information to investors and other key stakeholders, tailoring content for both technical and non-technical audiences.

Where I am now

I am a Senior Technical Writer specialising in API documentation, developer enablement, and technical content strategy. Since 2022, I have been the sole technical writer at Creditsafe, leading the transformation of our API documentation to enhance customer experience and streamline developer integration.

My Journey at Creditsafe

Creditsafe is a leading global provider of business credit risk and compliance solutions, with a database covering over 430 million businesses across more than 200 countries.

Upon joining Creditsafe, I encountered minimal and unmanaged API documentation. Recognising the critical need for comprehensive, user-centric documentation, I took full ownership of the project. My approach is rooted in a customer-first philosophy: understanding client needs, assessing current development stages, and bridging the gap with clear, actionable knowledge.

Key Achievements:

  • Comprehensive Documentation Overhaul:

    Independently audited and tested every API endpoint from a customer's perspective. Collaborated with product owners and development teams to ensure accuracy and relevance, creating all missing documentation single-handedly.
  • Customer-Centric API Portal:

    Designed and developed a new API documentation portal that segments the API into individual products. Features include:
    • Interactive in-browser testing
    • Downloadable OpenAPI specifications
    • Multi-language code examples
    • Process flows
    • Product Specific Guides
    • Integrated feedback mechanisms to identify and address customer pain points
    This initiative has significantly reduced customer inquiries, empowering users to integrate seamlessly without additional support.
  • Doc-as-Code Implementation:

    Transitioned documentation to a Doc-as-Code approach, integrating it into the CI/CD pipeline. This ensures real-time updates and maintains alignment with ongoing software developments.
  • Internal Knowledge Empowerment:

    Established a self-service API catalog for internal teams, standardising documentation processes and reducing dependencies on direct developer interactions.

Strategic Leadership

In my role, I engage with executive leadership to align documentation initiatives with Creditsafe's overarching business objectives. By collaborating closely with cross-functional teams, I ensure that our documentation strategy supports product development and enhances customer satisfaction. This alignment has positioned our documentation as a strategic asset, driving product adoption and contributing to business growth.

Approach & Philosophy

I believe that exceptional documentation is the cornerstone of a positive user experience. By immersing myself in the user journey, I ensure that our documentation is not only informative but also intuitive and accessible. My goal is to make our API offerings fully self-service, minimising the need for external support.

Future Goals

I am committed to continuous improvement and innovation. My future objectives include:

  • Enhancing developer experience (DX)
  • Integrating AI-driven solutions into documentation practices
  • Conducting API usability research to further refine our offerings

I thrive on bridging the gap between complex technical concepts and user-friendly content, ensuring that our documentation serves as a powerful tool for both customers and developers.
